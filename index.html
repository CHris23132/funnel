About Christopher

Self-taught web entrepreneur and developer. Christopher has been a self-directed learner dating back to the mastery of music production back in his early career as an 18-year-old operating Pro Tools at a music studio in Toronto. From there, he was hired by an animation studio to create animations and 3D scenes for an animated show in Blender 3D.

Chris found his true medium in development when he first set out to build an AR application inspired by his love for stop-motion animation. This led him into iOS Xcode development and eventually into full financial independence from web-based projects and remote work.

Chris has started a few app startups such as Stop Motion AR and Margaret, both iOS apps currently available on the App Store with paying annual users. Chris created a formula to start with and use a plan and framework to learn how to pick up useful skills such as web development, automation, workflows and app development.

For Christopher, it all started with learning the core concepts taught in the book and course. Don't believe me? This website was built following the proven method and Christopher guarantees this method - the same success can be yours today.
